Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon milk
  • ½ teaspoon minced garlic
  • salt to taste
  • ½ teaspoon pepper
  • 1 cup vegetable oil for frying
  • 8 thin cut boneless pork chops
  • 1 ½ cups panko crumbs

Steps

  1. Mix the eggs
  2. Dip in the egg mixture
  3. Coat with panko
  4. Fry until golden brown
